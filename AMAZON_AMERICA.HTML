AMAZON

What we love about the Amazon is that there is always an adventure. Anywhere you go, you’ll take a different method of transport between small boats to luxury ones or even kayaks.
Our favourite way to get out and enjoy the Amazon adventure is to use tandem kayaks


Plaza de Armas de Iquitos

Head to the port city of Iquitos in Peru for a fascinating visit, including a stroll around the Plaza de Armas. This busy central plaza sits among European-styled buildings built during the
historic rubber boom. Relax here in between your trips to the jungle and shopping along with the open-street markets.
Sit beside the fountain, admire the public garden, enjoy the shade of a palm tree, or check out the monument to those Peruvians who died during the 1879-83 Pacific War.



Tambopata National Reserve

Take your time admiring the delicate orchids, grassy ferns, vibrant bromeliads, and various twisting and tangled vines. You can access the reserve from the city of Puerto Maldonado
in the southeastern part of Peru.



Pacaya Samiria National Reserve

 Wildlife lives in the tops of the trees here, and you’ll be able to get a closer look at them. If you’re feeling particularly brave or looking to experience an extra thrill, make your way
across the La Posado canopy bridge for a spectacular view of this diverse ecosystem.
